Data Visualizations

Sunset Color Palettes

A Python-generated visualization analyzing the color distribution of 30 sunset photographs taken at Golden Gardens, Seattle. The x-axis represents capture date and the y-axis shows proportional color presence.

Pike Place Yelp Dendrogram

A hierarchical clustering of Yelp data visualizing points of interest in Pike Place Market. Node size reflects review ratings, revealing popularity patterns across categories.

Seattle Bike Traffic Flow

A Sankey diagram illustrating bike traffic volume between major trails and stops in Seattle. Line thickness corresponds to traffic magnitude.
